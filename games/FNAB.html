How To Play
Survive from 12 AM to 6 AM.
Four animatronics roam the building — keep them out.

📷 Cameras [TAB] — Watch the feeds. Click rooms on the map to switch.
◀ ▶ Doors — Block hallway attackers. Drains power while closed.
💡 Lights — Flash to see who's lurking at your door.
⬡ Vents — Seal vents to stop crawlers. Appears night 2+.

Power is limited. Every action drains it. No power = no defense.
Each night is harder. Listen for footsteps — they signal movement.
